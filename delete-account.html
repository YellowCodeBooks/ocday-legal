Delete Your OCDay Account & Data

OCDay, developed by Yellow Smart Lab, allows you to request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

How to request account deletion

  1. Send an email to yellowcode.books@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Delete Account Request"
  3. Send the email from your registered account email address, so we can verify your identity
  4. In the email body, please confirm you want your OCDay account and data permanently deleted
Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted within 2 business days of receiving your verified request.

What data is deleted

Upon a verified deletion request, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Your account profile (email, name)
  • All tasks and task-related data
  • App usage data and personal settings

What data may be retained

Subscription and billing records may be retained as required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention, tax compliance), in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

Need help?

If you have any questions about this process, feel free to reach out to yellowcode.books@gmail.com.
