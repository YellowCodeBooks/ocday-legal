Delete Your OCDay Account & Data

OCDay, developed by Yellow Smart Lab, lets you request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

How to request account deletion

  1. Open the OCDay app
  2. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account
  3. Confirm the deletion request

Alternatively, you can email us at yellowcode.books@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete Account Request" from your registered email address.

What data is deleted

Upon deletion request, the following data will be permanently deleted within 30 days:

  • Your account profile (email, name)
  • All tasks and task-related data
  • App usage and settings data

What data may be retained

Subscription and billing records may be retained as required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention, tax compliance), in accordance with our Privacy Policy.
